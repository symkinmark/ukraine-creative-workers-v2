## What this figure shows

Stacked bar chart showing, for each migration group, the proportion of individuals with confirmed death dates (event_observed=1) versus those right-censored as alive in 2026 (event_observed=0). Extended dataset: N=15,218.

- Red = Dead (confirmed death date in ESU)
- Blue/grey = Alive (right-censored at current age, 2026)

## Interpretation

The structural censoring imbalance is visually obvious: only non-migrated individuals have any censored observations. This explains why the right-censored Cox model cannot produce directly comparable HRs to the complete-case model — the reference group has a fundamentally different structure from all other groups.
