## What this figure shows

A simplified version of Figure 19 that reduces visual complexity by combining the non-migrated, internal transfer, and deported groups into a single "stayed in Soviet sphere" composite line. Shows three lines:

1. Migrated (emigrated from Soviet sphere)
2. Non-migrated (stayed in USSR, did not transfer)
3. Stayed in Soviet sphere (composite: non-migrated + internal transfer + deported)

Y-axis = normalised annual death rate (deaths per year as % of group size), 1921–1992.

---

## Key values

The 1937 spike in the stayed-in-Soviet-sphere composite reaches approximately 1.2% of the group's total size in a single year — far above the migrant trajectory which stays below 0.3% throughout the same period.
