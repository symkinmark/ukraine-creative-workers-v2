# Fig 05 — Deported Group: Age at Death Histogram

**File:** `charts/fig05_deported_age_histogram.png`

## What this chart shows
A histogram zoomed in on just the deported group (n=75). Shows how many people in this group died at each age band. The mean is marked with a vertical dashed line.

## Key finding
Mean age at death = **48.5 years** for the deported group. The distribution is not normal — it peaks in the 40s-50s and falls off sharply. There are very few deported individuals who survived into their 70s-80s.

## What to look for
- The peak of deaths is in the 40s-50s
- The right side (old age) is thin — few deportees survived long enough to die naturally in old age
- The mean line at 48.5 is pulled slightly right by the few long-lived survivors

## Why a separate chart for deported?
The deported group's distribution is so different from the others that it gets lost when shown together. This chart lets readers see the shape clearly without the scale being distorted by the larger groups.
