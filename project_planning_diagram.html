Project Planning Worksheet

Step 2 — Project Vision & Goals
What is your project idea?
Write a short summary of your idea — what you want to build or create.
What does success look like?
Describe the end result. What will exist when this project is done?
What makes this valuable or meaningful to you?
Why does this matter? Who benefits? What problem does it solve?
Step 3 — Task Breakdown & Delegation
For each major task in your project, answer the questions below. Add more rows as needed.
What are the major tasks needed to complete this project?
List each task — one per line. (e.g. research, writing, design, coding)
What skills or knowledge does each task need?
Think about what expertise, tools, or context each task requires.
Which parts need uniquely human strengths?
Judgment calls, relationship-building, lived experience, creativity, ethics.
Which parts could AI handle well?
Research, drafting, summarising, formatting, generating options, repetitive tasks.
Where would human + AI collaboration have the most impact?
Where does AI amplify your work rather than replace your thinking?
Reflection
What insights came out of your planning conversation?
Any surprises? Did your idea change or get clearer?
What part of your delegation plan will be hardest?
Where do you feel unsure or see potential problems?
What extra info or skills would help you delegate to AI better?
What do you need to learn, gather, or clarify before you start?
